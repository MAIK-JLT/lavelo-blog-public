# Image Prompt

Create a dynamic, scientific infographic showing a cyclist climbing a steep mountain road. Split the composition: left side shows a cyclist pedaling at low cadence (60 RPM) with powerful leg muscles highlighted in red, right side shows high cadence (90 RPM) with efficient cardiovascular flow in blue. Include a heart rate monitor and RPM gauge overlaid. Use a modern color palette of deep blues, energetic oranges, and vibrant greens. Incorporate data visualization elements: cadence curves, energy efficiency graphs, and muscle fiber activation diagrams. The cyclist should be in professional gear on a road bike, with realistic musculature and anatomical detail. Background: dramatic mountain landscape with climbing sun. Style: blend photorealism with modern sports science illustration, clean typography, professional cycling magazine aesthetic.
